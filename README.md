## Project Overview / 專案概要

**Research Question:** To what extent does school-induced fear (days skipped due to safety concerns) predict adolescent defensive violence and weapon carrying behaviors, after controlling for demographic background and underlying depressive trauma?

**Methodology:** Ordinary Least Squares (OLS) Multiple Linear Regression.

**Summary:** Utilizing the 2007 National Youth Risk Behavior Survey (YRBS) dataset, this project investigates how "school insecurity" predicts a student's "comprehensive violence and weapon risk profile." The raw data was cleaned and recoded into a continuous scale of environmental fear and a composite index of violent behaviors, while explicitly controlling for biological sex and prolonged depressive affect. The empirical results demonstrate a significant positive correlation between school fear and violence risk, highlighting a highly volatile "perfect storm" when environmental fear is combined with internal depression, particularly among male students.

**研究問題：** 在控制了人口統計背景與潛在的憂鬱創傷後，因校園安全疑慮而缺課的天數，能在多大程度上顯著預測青少年的防禦性暴力與攜帶武器行為？

**統計方法：** 最小平方法（OLS）多變數線性迴歸。

**總結：** 本專案使用 2007 年美國青少年危險行為調查（YRBS）數據，探討「校園不安全感」如何預測學生的「綜合暴力與武器風險行為」。數據經過精準清理與重新編碼，轉換為連續型的環境恐懼尺度與綜合暴力風險指數，並引入生理性別與長期憂鬱絕望感作為控制變數。實證結果顯示，校園恐懼與暴力風險之間存在顯著的正相關，並凸顯了當外在環境恐懼與內在憂鬱創傷結合時（特別是在男學生群體中），將引發極高風險的「完美風暴」。
